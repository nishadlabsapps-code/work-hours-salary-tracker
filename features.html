Features

Premium tools designed to make work, attendance, and salary tracking feel easy.

Every feature is built to reduce confusion, help workers trust their totals, and make reports simple to review or share.

Work Hours Tracking

Track daily work time with flexible options that fit hourly jobs, day-based jobs, and shift routines.

Attendance Tracking

Keep every attendance entry visible so working days, absences, and patterns stay easy to understand.

Smart Salary Calculator

Automatically estimate salary from time worked, shifts, rates, overtime, and attendance data.

Overtime Earnings

Add overtime hours and instantly see the extra income reflected in total earnings.

Monthly & Detailed Reports

Review salary summaries with line-by-line visibility into hours, attendance, overtime, and totals.

Attendance History

Go back through saved records and see work history clearly without hunting through spreadsheets.

Calendar View

See work days visually in a calendar layout that helps patterns stand out immediately.

Edit / Delete Records

Adjust saved entries quickly whenever shifts change, hours update, or corrections are needed.

Export & Share (PDF & HTML)

Create polished reports and share them instantly via WhatsApp or any other app when proof matters.

Cloud Backup with Google Login

Keep your experience connected with a backup-ready account flow and Google-based access.

Profile Customization

Set names, work details, and personal preferences so the app reflects your own routine.

Clean UI

A focused interface helps you move faster, read reports clearly, and avoid clutter during daily use.
